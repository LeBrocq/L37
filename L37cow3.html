This calculator is designed to provide some reassurance when everything feels a little uncertain. And that there are some considered steps you can take before making a full commitment.

Costs impacted by delays are mostly due to timing, construction cost escalation, holding costs and temporary accommodation. There are also some practical next steps depending on where you are in the journey.

Construction costs escalation
Modelled as a range from 1.0% to 2.0% annually.
Holding costs
Land finance assumed at 5.5% to 6.5% with council-related holding rates of 0.1% to 0.4% annually.
Temporary accommodation
Rental allowance modelled at $1,000 per week where relevant.
Market references
Assumptions reflect available market data as at April 2026. Sourced from Cotality, Canstar, the Essential Services Commission, and realestate.com.au.
