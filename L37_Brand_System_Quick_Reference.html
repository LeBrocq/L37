Brand system, quick reference

The working tokens of the Latitude 37 identity, distilled to be applied, not admired. The system pairs warm, tactile neutrals (the human) with confident, functional type (the engineered), held together by the circle and square.

03

Organic geometry

The system's spine. Derived from the relationship between circle and square, these elements appear individually or combined, in light or reverse, as a flexible graphic language.

Circle
The human side. Relationships, intuition, the individual needs of every client. Hand-drawn, warm, a little imperfect.
Square
The engineered side. Structure, precision, the disciplined thinking that delivers. Confident, rigorous, certain.
Circle + Square
The two together. Empathy and expertise, creativity and discipline, people and process. The integrated L37 approach.
04

Using it well

  • Let warm neutrals own the surfaces. Bone and Paper ground most layouts; Soft and Grey add quiet layering.
  • Set type in Charcoal, drop to Grey Lead for secondary text and labels. Reserve white for reverse fields.
  • Treat Biro as a single, deliberate accent. It belongs to human moments: a quote, an annotation, one structural highlight. If it appears more than once or twice on a page, pull it back.
  • Pair the voices on purpose. Merlo for editorial weight, Steradian for utility and labels, September Spirit for the human hand, Roboto Mono for data and calls to action.
  • Use the circle and square as a system, not a decoration. Each mark should carry the meaning it stands for, human or engineered, not sit in as a graphic flourish.
  • Avoid the retired palette. Cream, brown, terracotta and navy belong to earlier work and read as off-brand against V2.0.
  • Avoid the long dash. House style sets em and en dashes aside in favour of commas, colons and full stops.
05

Tokens

Drop-in values for code and for briefing a designer. Add the licensed font files to a /fonts folder to make the type true.

:root{
  /* base neutrals — human */
  --bone:#EAE4DA; --paper:#EDECE9; --grey:#F1F2F2; --soft:#F7F7F7;
  /* toned typography — engineered */
  --charcoal:#231F20; --grey-lead:#4B4E53; --biro:#0040CC;
  /* type roles */
  --serif:'Merlo Original', Georgia, serif;        /* H1–H5, body */
  --sans:'Steradian', Arial, sans-serif;          /* subheads, labels */
  --hand:'September Spirit', cursive;             /* quotes, in Biro */
  --mono:'Roboto Mono', monospace;               /* CTAs, data */
  /* scale — golden ratio 1.309 (size/leading) */
  --h1:80/120; --h2:47/72; --h3:36/56; --h4:27/48; --h5:21/32; --body:16/160%;
}
